## Language & Tone

- **English-first, Japanese-inspired.** Keep the app accessible to non-Japanese speakers while preserving the Japanese cleaning traditions that inspire it.
- Japanese text is welcome as *flavor* — short subtitles, recipe Japanese names, traditional method references — but never as the primary language.
- If you remove Japanese text, only remove it when it makes a screen feel bilingual rather than accentuated. A line like `片付け · Katazuke` is fine as a subtitle; a full Japanese description is not.
- Same rule for icons/emoji: they're fine as accents, don't overdo it.
